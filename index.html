Visual comparison of online survey demographics

Online surveys are an important method in empirical software engineering research to investigate the state of practice. As drawing a random sample of a certain population of software developers is rarely possible, researchers often rely on so-called convenience samples with limited external validity. To enable researchers to compare own online survey samples with existing studies such as the annual Stack Overflow developer survey, we will develop a web tool that allows researchers to upload key demographics of their study participants (e.g., age, work experience, gender). The tool will then automatically compare the researcher's sample with suitable existing studies. We utilise different visualisations to support users in identifying salient differences
